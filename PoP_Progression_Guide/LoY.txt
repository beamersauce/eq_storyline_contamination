<a href="file:///storyline/storyPlanesofPowerProgression.txt">----Spells in Legacy of Ykesha----</a><br>
<br>
Legacy of Ykesha releases one hour after Planes of Power.  The spells are quested in LoY zones.<br>
Some spells are lower level and useless, while others like Temperance, Pure Blood, Blood of Nadox, and the bard Arias are very useful.<br>
