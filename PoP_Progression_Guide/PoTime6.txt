<c "#fc7703">  < > < > Boss Info < > < > < > </c><br>
Name: Quarm<br>
Level: 80<br>
HP: 1750,000+<br>
Max Hit: 4000<br>

<c "#0080ff">  < > < > Event Info < > < > < > </c><br>
AE rampage, slowable, flurries.  Every 25% Quarm loses a head, dispels himself (re-debuff!) and loses some AEs. Burn him from the start to make the fight easier.<br>
Chain celestial regen, wood, and totems all need to be used to keep everyone up.<br>
A time vortex mobs spawn periodically and must be mezzed.  Knights should try to quickly get aggro (without damaging them) so they don't hit melee (they proc a curable slow, noted below).<br>
Time Warp (Unresistable): Range 200 single target 50% spell slow, 50% melee slow, lasts 60 seconds, 36 disease counters.  Cure this!<br>
Buff all resists.<br>

<c "#0080ff">  < > < > Spells Info < > < > < > </c><br>
Balance of the Nameless (Self-buff): Cleanses Quarm of debuffs every 25%.<br>
Plague Seism (-250 Magic): Range 400 PBAE 3000 damage.<br>
Epoch Conviction (-300 Magic): Range 400 PBAE 100/tick DoT, 50/tick mana drain, -280 ATK, decrease spell dmg 20%, lasts 1 minute, 36 curse counters.<br>
Glacier Blast (-250 Cold): Range 400 PBAE 3000 damage.<br>
Glacier Breath (-300 Cold): Range 400 PBAE 100/tick DoT, 20% snare).<br>
Infernal Flames (-250 Fire): Range 400 PBAE 3000 damage.<br>
Plagued Earth (-300 Disease): Range 400 PBAE 500/tick DoT, 50/tick mana drain, lasts 1.5 minutes, 36 disease counters.<br>
Venom Blast (-250 Poison): Range 400 PBAE 3000 damage.<br>
Venomed Mist (-300 Poison): Range 400 PBAE 500/tick DoT, 50/tick mana drain, lasts 1.5 minutes, 36 poison counters.<br>
