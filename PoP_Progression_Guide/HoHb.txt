Name: Lord Mithaniel Marr<br>
Level: 77<br>
HP: 1,200,000<br>
Max Hit: 2000+<br>
<c "#0080ff">  < > < > Event Info < > < > < > </c><br>
In order to target and kill LMM, you must kill (in order) Halon, Edium, and Ralthazor.<br>
Single target ramp, AE Ramp, flurries, slowable (disease).<br>
Buff magic resist.<br>
Strong vs. all spells.<br>
Dog adds respawn after 12 minutes, so time your clearing so you don't get respawns mid-fight.<br>
<c "#0080ff">  < > < > Spells Info < > < > < > </c><br>
Strike of  Marr (-200 Magic): Single target 3500 DD, 3 second stun.<br>
Silence of Marr (-200 Magic): Root.
