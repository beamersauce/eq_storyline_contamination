<a href="file:///storyline/storyPlanesofPowerProgression.txt">---- Spells in Planes of Power ----</a><br>
Lower level spells and ports can be purchased from the library in PoK (Use Find)</c><br>
Level 61-65 spells are obtained randomly by turning in parchments or glyphed rune words to specific class NPCs.<br>
<br>
<c "#cfcf00">Bards:</c> <a href="https://everquest.allakhazam.com/db/npc.html?id=10087>Minstrel Eoweril</a> in PoK (Bard Songs 1-25).<br>
<c "#42b0f5">Beastlords:</c> <a href="https://everquest.allakhazam.com/db/npc.html?id=10173>Primalist Saosith</a> in PoK (Beastlord Spells 1-25).<br>
<c "#0080ff">Clerics:</c> <a href="https://everquest.allakhazam.com/db/npc.html?id=10180>Vicar Ceraen</a> in PoK (Cleric Spells 1-25).<br>
<c "#00f000">Druids:</c> <a href="https://everquest.allakhazam.com/db/npc.html?id=10188>Wanderer Astobin</a> in PoK (Druid Spells 1-25).<br>
<c "#f5f500">Enchanters:</c> <a href="https://everquest.allakhazam.com/db/npc.html?id=10083>Illusionist Jerup</a> in PoK (Enchanter Spells 1-25).<br>
<c "#8f4800">Magicians:</c> <a href="https://everquest.allakhazam.com/db/npc.html?id=10155>Elementalist Somat</a> in PoK (Magician Spells 1-25).<br>
<c "#7300a0">Necromancers:</c> <a href="https://everquest.allakhazam.com/db/npc.html?id=10089>Heretic Drahur</a> in PoK (Necromancer Spells 1-25).<br>
<c "#50a192">Paladins:</c> <a href="https://everquest.allakhazam.com/db/npc.html?id=10168>Cavalier Waut</a> in PoK (Paladin Spells 1-25).<br>
<c "#f07f00">Rangers:</c> <a href="https://everquest.allakhazam.com/db/npc.html?id=10380>Pathfinder Viliken</a> in PoK (Ranger Spells 1-25).<br>
<c "#7f0000">Shadowknights:</c> <a href="https://everquest.allakhazam.com/db/npc.html?id=10094>Reaver Nydlil</a> in PoK (Shadowknight Spells 1-25).<br>
<c "#c2b459">Shamans:</c> <a href="https://everquest.allakhazam.com/db/npc.html?id=13937>Mystic Abomin</a> in PoK (Shaman Spells 1-25).<br>
<c "#c045ff">Wizards:</c> <a href="https://everquest.allakhazam.com/db/npc.html?id=10078>Channeler Olaemos</a> in PoK (Wizard Spells 1-25).<br>
<br>
<c "#0080ff"><a href="https://everquest.allakhazam.com/db/item.html?item=16329>Ethereal Parchments</a> (61 and 62 Spells) are dropped from Tier 1, Tier 2, and Tier 3 zones.</c><br>
<c "#0080ff"><a href="https://everquest.allakhazam.com/db/item.html?item=16493>Spectral Parchments</a> (63 and 64 Spells) are dropped from some group content and also raid content and bosses.</c><br>
<c "#0080ff"><a href="https://everquest.allakhazam.com/db/item.html?item=16562>Glyphed Rune Words</a> (65 Spells) are dropped from T2 or higher raid content and bosses.</c><br>
<br>
<c "#00f000">Druid</c> Portal Spells are from:<br>
 - <a href="https://everquest.allakhazam.com/db/npc.html?id=10188>Wanderer Astobin</a> (Druid Spells 1-25) in the Plane of Knowledge Library - Main Floor, south side.<br>
 - <a href="https://everquest.allakhazam.com/db/npc.html?id=10185>Wanderer Qenda</a> (Druid Spells 25-60) in the Plane of Knowledge Library - Main Floor, southwest side.<br>
 - <a href="https://everquest.allakhazam.com/db/npc.html?id=7509>Ealerel Tidebringer</a> (Spell Merchant - Plane of Tranquility Ports) in Shadow Haven, in the Beastlord Guild area in the basement (use teleport pad).<br>
<br>
<c "#c045ff">Wizard</c> Portal Spells are from:<br>
 - <a href="https://everquest.allakhazam.com/db/npc.html?id=10082>Channeler Lariland</a> (Wizard Spells 26-50) in the Plane of Knowledge Library - 2nd basement floor,  east side.<br>
  - <a href="https://everquest.allakhazam.com/db/npc.html?id=10078>Channeler Olaemons (LoY Ports)</a> (Wizard Spells 1-25) in the Plane of Knowledge Library - 2nd basement floor,  east side.<br>
   - <a href="https://everquest.allakhazam.com/db/npc.html?id=7804>Goesl Sealsonich</a> (Spell Merchant - Plane of Tranquility Ports) in Shadow Haven, in the library in the far northeast corner of the zone.<br>
 <br>
 <c "#0080ff">Melee Tomes</c><br>
 <c "#e8bc0c">Warrior: Incite and Spirit of Rage:</c> <a href="https://everquest.allakhazam.com/db/npc.html?id=13820>Heldin Swordbreaker</a> (Warrior Tomes) in Plane of Knowledge library, 1st basement floor, southwest side.<br>
 <c "#9e5a02">Monk: Phantom Echo:</c> <a href="https://everquest.allakhazam.com/db/npc.html?id=13961>Beorobin Amondson</a> (Monk Tomes) in Plane of Knowledge library, southeast side.<br>
 <c "#dde02d">Rogue: Assassin's Strike and Deadly Precision:</c> <a href="https://everquest.allakhazam.com/db/npc.html?id=15759>Blade Darkblade</a> (Rogue Tomes) in Plane of Knowledge library, 2nd floor, south side.<br>
