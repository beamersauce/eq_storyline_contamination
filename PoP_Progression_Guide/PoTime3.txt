<a href="https://www.raspersrealm.com/Everquest/PoP/PoTime_3.html">---- Time Phase 3 Bosses: Rasper's ----</a><br>
<br>
Once all phase 2 trials are complete, move through the clock, INVIS, and jump off the cliff.  Run straight and camp/med near the large blue portal for phase 3.<br>
Each of the 16 mini bosses and the two bosses in Phase 3 can drop <a href="https://everquest.allakhazam.com/db/item.html?item=33307">Time Phased Quintessence</a>, the backflag for Plane of Time (does not enhance charm).<br>
There are 8 waves that become active one at a time (when the previous wave's two named are killed).<br>
Each wave consists of around 8 mobs or so + 2 named.  Trash hits for max 1000+, named max hit 2000+.<br>
Once everything but one or two mobs is killed, the raid should move down and to the side opposite of the last living mob.  When it dies, the two bosses (rock golems) will spawn.  Split and kill them one by one (Supernatural Guardian flurries and is unslowable).<br>
<br>
Wave 1. Zek army. NAMED: Deathbringer Blackheart, Ferocious Warboar.<br>
Wave 2. Bertox army. NAMED: Kraksmaal Fir`dethsin, Xeroan Xi`Geruon.<br>
Wave 3. Zek army. Named: Deathbringer Skullsmash, Deadly Warboar.<br>
Wave 4. Cazic Thule army. Named: Herlsoakian, Sinrunal Gorgedreal.<br>
Wave 5. Zek army. Named: Deathbringer Rianit, Needletusk Warboar.<br>
Wave 6. Innoruuk army. Named: Dersool Fal`Giersnaol, Xerskel Gerodnsal.<br>
Wave 7. Terris Thule army. Named: Dark Knight of Terris, Undead Squad Leader.<br>
Wave 8. Saryrn army. Named: Dreamwarp, Champion of Torment.<br>
