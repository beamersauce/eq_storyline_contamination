<br>
- You can preloot all items for Aiding Askr (BoT Access).<br>
- When finished, you do not have to zone into BoT from Plane of Storms.  You can zone from Tranquility.<br>
- Named are one groupable, but have 175k HP, and drop 6-7 medallions.<br>
- You need the following:<br>
1 Storm giant head<br>
1 Storm Satuur Sash (NE camp)<br>
1 Storm Taarid Bone (West camp)<br>
1 Volaas Beard (South camp)<br>
2 Esoteric Medallions (from different camps)<br>
