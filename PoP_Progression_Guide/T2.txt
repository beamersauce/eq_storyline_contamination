<br>
Guide Format:<br>
<c "#00ff44">Green steps are NPC dialogue or zoning related,</c><br>
<c "#ff3b3b">Red steps are boss kills/fights.</c><br>
<c "#c073ff">Purple steps are not "required" flags. Only enough people need to do these to keep your raid moving forward.</c><br>

<c "#0080ff">Ruins of Lxanvom (Crypt of Decay) </c> <a href="https://everquest.allakhazam.com/db/zones.html?zstrat=174> - <a href="file:///storyline/PoP_Progression_Guide/CoD.txt> CoD Story Window Boss Guide</a> - Zone Page</a> <a href="https://www.raspersrealm.com/Everquest/PoP/CoD_Events.html> - CoD Events Page</a><br>
<c "#00ff44">[_] Hail Elder Fuirstel in Plane of Tranquility in the Sick Bay.</c> *Must have completed Grummus Flag to get a response.* <c "#fbff00">(You've received a character flag!)</c><br>
<c "#ff3b3b>[_] Complete Carprin Event and hail Tarkil Adan in Crypt of Decay to gain access to lower floor. </c>(Keyring: Key to the lower depths of the Ruins of Lxanvom). <c "#fbff00">(You've received a character flag!)</c><br>
<c "#ff3b3b>[_] Complete Bertoxxulous Event in Crypt of Decay (lower floor) and hail A Planar Projection. </c> <c "#fbff00">(You've received a character flag!)</c><br>
<c "#00ff44">[_] Hail Elder Fuirstel again in Plane of Tranquility in the Sick Bay.</c> <c "#fbff00">(You've received a character flag!)</c><br>
<c "#00ff44">[_] Hail Adler Fuirstel again outside Plane of Disease.</c><br>
<br>
<c "#0080ff">Plane of Torment</c> <a href="https://everquest.allakhazam.com/db/zones.html?zstrat=167> - <a href="file:///storyline/PoP_Progression_Guide/PoT.txt> PoT Story Window Boss Guide</a> - Zone Page</a> <a href="https://www.raspersrealm.com/Everquest/PoP/PoTorm_Events.html> - Plane of Torment Events</a><br>
Note: To be able to speak to the NPCs in Plane of Tranquility to start the Torment flagging process, you need to have completed Plane of Disease/Crypt of Decay/Plane of Nightmare flags correctly.<br>
You can still kill Keeper of Sorrows and Saryrn ahead of time if you get piggied into Plane of Torment but you'll need to fix your flags afterwards.<br><br>
<c "#00ff44">[_] Say "i will go" to Fahlia Shadyglade in Plane of Tranquility.  She is in the Sick Bay. </c> <c "#fbff00">(You've received a character flag!)</c><br>
<c "#c073ff">[_] Optional - Quest for <a href="https://everquest.allakhazam.com/db/quest.html?quest=2295> - Screaming Sphere (key)</a> to open the door to Saryrn's Tower in Plane of Torment.  Only one person needs this. </c><br>
<c "#ff3b3b>[_] Complete the Keeper of Sorrows Event in Plane of Torment and hail Tylis Newleaf who spawns at the end of the event. <c "#fbff00">(You've received a character flag!)</c><br>
<c "#ff3b3b>[_] Kill Saryrn in Plane of Torment and hail A Planar Projection. </c> <c "#fbff00">(You've received a character flag!)</c><br>
<c "#00ff44">[_] Go to Plane of Tranquility and hail both Fahlia Shadyglade and Tylis Newleaf in the Sick Bay.</c><br>
<br>
<c "#0080ff">Plane of Storms</c> <a href="https://everquest.allakhazam.com/db/zones.html?zstrat=172> - <a href="file:///storyline/PoP_Progression_Guide/PoS.txt> PoS Story Window Boss Guide</a> - Zone Page</a> <a href="https://www.raspersrealm.com/Everquest/PoP/PoS_Overview.html> - PoStorms Map and Overview</a> <a href="https://www.raspersrealm.com/Everquest/PoP/PoS_Events.html> - PoStorms Events Page</a> <br>
<c "#ff3b3b>[_] Complete Askr the Lost's <a href="https://everquest.allakhazam.com/db/quest.html?quest=2269> - Aiding Askr</a> quest (Bastion of Thunder access) </c>(Keyring: Talisman of Thunderous Foyer). <c "#fbff00">(You've received a character flag!)</c><br>
<c "#00ff44">[_] Zone into Bastion of Thunder via stone in the middle of the Plane of Storms zone OR Plane of Tranquility. </c><c "#fbff00">(You've received a character flag!)</c><br>
<br>
<c "#0080ff">Plane of Valor</c> <a href="https://everquest.allakhazam.com/db/zones.html?zstrat=170> - <a href="file:///storyline/PoP_Progression_Guide/PoV.txt> PoV Story Window Boss Guide</a> - Zone Page</a> <a href="https://www.raspersrealm.com/Everquest/PoP/PoV_Events.html> - PoValor Events Page</a> <br>
<c "#c073ff">[_] Optional - Obtain <a href="https://everquest.allakhazam.com/db/quest.html?quest=2481> - Crystalline Globe (key)</a> to open the door to Aerin`Dar.  Only one person needs this. </c><br>
<c "#ff3b3b>[_] Kill Aerin`Dar and hail A Planar Projection. </c><c "#fbff00">(You've received a character flag!)</c><br>
<c "#00ff44">[_] Zone into Halls of Honor via the Plane of Valor underground tunnel entrance. </c><c "#fbff00">(You've received a character flag!)</c><br>
