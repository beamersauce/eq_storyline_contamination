<c "#fc7703">  < > < > Boss Info < > < > < > </c><br>
Name: Agnarr the Storm Lord<br>
Level: 75<br>
HP: 1,100,000<br>
Max Hit: 1200+<br>
<c "#0080ff">  < > < > Event Info < > < > < > </c><br>
Slowable.<br>
Buff magic resist.<br>
There are three phases in this fight.  Fire Room, Lightning Room, and Agnarr's room.<br>
In all three rooms, destroy the portals first to stop adds, then kill boss.<br>
Agnarr spawns a new named mob every 25%.<br>
<c "#0080ff">  < > < > Spells Info < > < > < > </c><br>
Manastorm (Magic): Range 150 PBAE 600 DD, 200 mana drain.<br>
Rage of the Rainkeeper (Unresistable): Range 50 PBAE 800 DD, 80/tick DoT, 7.5 sec spinstun.
