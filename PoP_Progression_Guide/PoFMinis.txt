<c "#0080ff">Quick note:</c> Everything in Plane of Fire is immune to fire (+10,000 Fire Resist).<br>
Almost all bosses are similarly resistant to all other spell types, with the exception of Magmaton, who is highly resistant to magic (+1000).<br>

<c "#fc7703">  < > < > Boss Info < > < > < > </c><br>
Name: Fennin Ro, the Tyrant of Fire<br>
Level: 80<br>
HP: 950,000<br>
Max Hit: 1900+<br>

<c "#0080ff">  < > < > Event Info < > < > < > </c><br>
AE ramp, slowable.<br>
Buff fire resist.<br>
Slightly less resistant to disease than others.<br>
To begin this event, kill the Guardian of Doomfire (flurries) near Tables (after Yozanni and Reparm).  Go through the script, killing the mobs and named on the way to Fennin Ro.<br>
There are eight mini bosses in the script, which are detailed below.<br>

Melee should max range and back out once a minute to avoid the AE (there is a GINA trigger for this).  Once he dies, hail Essence of Fire to get your Globe of Dancing Flame.<br>
<c "#0080ff">  < > < > Spells Info < > < > < > </c><br>
Cataclysm of Ro (-1000 Fire): Range 100 PBAE 3000 damage, 100/tick DoT, 100/tick mana drain, lasts one hour. 18 curse counters.<br>
