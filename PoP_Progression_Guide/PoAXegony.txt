<c "#fc7703">  < > < > Boss Info < > < > < > </c><br>
Name: Xegony the Queen of Air<br>
Level: 80<br>
HP: 1,500,000<br>
Max Hit: 2000+<br>

<c "#0080ff">  < > < > Event Info < > < > < > </c><br>
AE rampage, flurries, slowable.<br>
Buff magic resist.<br>
Before engaging, pull all adds out without aggroing Xegony.<br>
Adds pop at 100% and around every 15% after until 25% (6 waves).  Disengage from Xegony and kill (random) waves of adds.<br>
Adds never despawn and can memblur, making kiting difficult.  Many, however, are mezzable.<br>
Knights, spiders and efreetis cast an AE silence (125 range, 45 second recast).<br>
Elementals, bugs, and phoenixes cast a targeted 1250 DD & AE slow (55%).<br>
<c "#0080ff">  < > < > Spells Info < > < > < > </c><br>
Winds of Xegony (-400 Magic): Range 100 PBAE 1000 damage, 200/tick DoT, 30% slow.  Lasts 1.8 minutes, 13 curse counters.<br>
