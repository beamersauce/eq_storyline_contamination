<c "#fc7703">  < > < > Boss Info < > < > < > </c><br>
Name: Aerin`Dar<br>
Level: 70<br>
HP: 300,000<br>
Max Hit: 1600+<br>
<c "#0080ff">  < > < > Event Info < > < > < > </c><br>
Single target ramp, slowable.<br>
Buff magic resist.<br>
Spawns numerous A Mindless Minion (mezzable, snareable, and rootable) and one Rahlgon (snareable) during the fight.<br>
Recommend knight tank due to being immune to fear.<br>
Paladins and other root classes should be able to easily lock down all the adds (or kill them).  Offtank named when it spawns and burn boss.<br>
<c "#0080ff">  < > < > Spells Info < > < > < > </c><br>
Shards of Glass (-50 Magic): Single target 375 DD.<br>
Glass Shards (-150 Magic): Single target 3000 damage and 45% slow.<br>
Glass Roar (-150 Magic): Range 200 PBAE 500/tick DoT, 700 damage DD, fear.
