<c "#fc7703">  < > < > Boss Info < > < > < > </c><br>
<a href="https://www.eqprogression.com/manaeticbehemonth/">---- Manaetic Bahemoth ----</a><br>
Level: 70<br>
HP: 175,000<br>
<c "#0080ff">  < > < > Event Info < > < > < > </c><br>
Most classes should stay outside room to avoid PBAEs (However, fight is very short).<br>
High resist/high DPS melee can go into room, along with tanks.<br>
Tanks/melee should buff all resists.<br>
Casters step into room and cast spells from just inside doors.  Can range most AEs from here.<br>
Kill spiders (slowable) as they spawn.  Prevent them from reaching boss.<br>
<c "#0080ff">  < > < > Spells Info < > < > < > </c><br>
Biomelt IX (-150 Disease): Targeted AE 900 damage.<br>
Deathfog IV (-150 Poison): PBAE 900 damage.<br>
Gas Leak (-150 Fire): PBAE 900 damage.<br>
Gyrosonic Disruption (-300 Magic): PBAE 600 damage.<br>
Liquid Hydrokill (-150 Cold): PBAE 900/tick DoT with snare.<br>
Mana Beam (Unresistable): Single target 2000 DD.<br>
Wave of White Noise (Unresistable): Silence.<br>
