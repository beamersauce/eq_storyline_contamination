<a href="file:///storyline/PoP_Progression_Guide/Flagging.txt"> ----Tier 4 (Elemental Planes) ----</a><br>
<c "#0080ff">Doomfire, the Burning Lands (Plane of Fire)</c> <a href="https://everquest.allakhazam.com/db/zones.html?zstrat=168> - <a href="file:///storyline/PoP_Progression_Guide/PoF.txt> PoF Story Window Boss Guide</a> - Zone Page</a> <a href="https://www.raspersrealm.com/Everquest/PoP/PoF_Events.html> - PoF Events Page</a><br>
<c "#ff3b3b>[_] Kill Fennin Ro, the Tyrant of Fire and hail Essence of Fire to receive <a href="https://everquest.allakhazam.com/db/item.html?item=20743> Globe of Dancing Flame</a>.</c><br>
<br>
<c "#0080ff">Eryslai, the Kingdom of Wind (Plane of Air)</c> <a href="https://everquest.allakhazam.com/db/zones.html?zstrat=175> - <a href="file:///storyline/PoP_Progression_Guide/PoA.txt> PoA Story Window Boss Guide</a> - Zone Page</a> <a href="https://www.raspersrealm.com/Everquest/PoP/PoA_Events.html> - PoA Events</a><br>
<c "#c073ff">[_] Optional - Obtain <a href="https://everquest.allakhazam.com/db/quest.html?quest=3021> - Wind Etched Key</a> to zone up to Xegony's Island.  The key only ports your group.</c><br>
<c "#ff3b3b>[_] Kill Xegony the Queen of Air and hail Essence of Air to receive <a href="https://everquest.allakhazam.com/db/item.html?item=20744> Amorphous Cloud of Air</a>.</c><br>
<br>
<c "#0080ff">Reef of Coirnav (Plane of Water)</c> <a href="https://everquest.allakhazam.com/db/zones.html?zstrat=166> - <a href="file:///storyline/PoP_Progression_Guide/PoW.txt> PoW Story Window Boss Guide</a> - Zone Page</a> <a href="https://www.raspersrealm.com/Everquest/PoP/PoW_Events.html> - PoW Events Page</a> <br>
<c "#ff3b3b>[_] Kill Coirnav the Avatar of Water and hail Essence of Water to receive <a href="https://everquest.allakhazam.com/db/item.html?item=19437> Sphere of Coalesced Water</a>.</c><br>
<br>
<c "#0080ff">Vegarlson, the Earthen Badlands (Plane of Earth)</c> <a href="https://everquest.allakhazam.com/db/zones.html?zstrat=169> - <a href="file:///storyline/PoP_Progression_Guide/PoE.txt> PoE Story Window Boss Guide</a> - Zone Page</a> <a href="https://www.raspersrealm.com/Everquest/PoP/PoE_Events.html> - PoE Events Page</a> <br>
<c "#c073ff">[_] Optional - Kill Tantisala Jaggedtooth in Plane of Earth A and loot <a href="https://everquest.allakhazam.com/db/item.html?item=28444> Gem-Etched Key</a>. One person needs this to open the door to the caves.</c><br>
<c "#ff3b3b>[_] Complete the four ring events in Plane of Earth A to spawn <a href="https://everquest.allakhazam.com/db/npc.html?id=12945> A Mystical Arbitor of Earth</a>.  Kill it and hail A Planar Projection to receive <c "#fbff00">Passkey of the Twelve</c> on your key ring. </c><c "#00ff44">After killing A Mystical Arbitor of Earth, click the door nearby to enter Plane of Earth B.</c><br>
<c "#ff3b3b>[_] Kill The Rathe Council in Plane of Earth B and hail Essence of Earth to receive <a href="https://everquest.allakhazam.com/db/item.html?item=20645> Mound of Living Stone</a>.</c><br>
