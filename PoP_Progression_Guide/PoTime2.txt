<a href="https://www.raspersrealm.com/Everquest/PoP/PoTime_2.html">---- Time Phase 2 Bosses: Rasper's ----</a><br>
<br>
You have the remaining time from phase 1 + one hour to complete phase 2.  Phase 2 is split into three areas: Fire/Water, Earth/Air, and Undead.<br>
Undead will stay and pull phase 2 mobs to the entrance, the other two groups can invis and move to their respective back clock further west.<br>
Clear the trash, clear the named, then once all trials are complete, you move through the clock, INVIS, and jump off the cliff.  Run straight and camp/med near the large blue portal for phase 3.<br>
