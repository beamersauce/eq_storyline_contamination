<c "#fc7703">  < > < > < > < > < > Flagging Paths  < > < > < > < > < > </c><br>
<c "#0080ff">To go straight to Air, Water, and Earth:</c><br>
1. Complete one trial in Plane of Justice.<br>
2. Complete Askr's quest in Plane of Storms for BoT access.<br>
3. Defeat Agnarr the Storm Lord in BoT.<br>
4. Defeat Manaetic Bahemoth in Plane of Innovation.<br>
5. Defeat Tallon Zek, Vallon Zek, and Rallos Zek the Warlord in Plane of Tactics.<br>
<br>
<c "#0080ff">To go straight to Plane of Fire:</c><br>
1. Complete one trial in Plane of Justice.<br>
2. Defeat Grummus in Plane of Disease.<br>
3. Defeat Manaetic Bahemoth in Plane of Innovation.<br>
4. Complete the Hedge event in PoN and defeat Terris Thule in PoNB.<br>
5. Defeat Bertoxxulous in Crypt of Decay.<br>
6. Defeat the Keeper of Sorrows and Saryrn in Plane of Torment.<br>
7. Defeat Aerin`Dar in Plane of Valor.<br>
8. Complete the three trials in Halls of Honor A.<br>
9. Defeat Lord Mithaniel Marr in Halls of Honor B.<br>
10. Defeat Tallon Zek, Vallon Zek, and Rallos Zek the Warlord in Plane of Tactics.<br>
11. Defeat the five minis and Solusek Ro in the Solusek Ro's Tower.<br>
