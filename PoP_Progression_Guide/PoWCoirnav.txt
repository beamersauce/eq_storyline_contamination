<c "#fc7703">  < > < > Boss Info < > < > < > </c><br>
Name: Coirnav the Avatar of Water<br>
Level: 80<br>
HP: 250,000<br>
Max Hit: 2100+<br>

<c "#0080ff">  < > < > Event Info < > < > < > </c><br>
Rampage and AE rampage, slowable?<br>
Buff cold resist.<br>
1. Kill Guardian of Coirnav.<br>
2. Kill little adds then kill Pwelon of Vapor (2.5 million HP and hit 1500+).<br>
3. Kill little adds and root Nrinda of Ice away from raid (2.5 million HP and hit 1500+).<br>
4. Kill little adds then kill Vamuil of Water (2.5 million HP and hit 1500+).<br>
5. Pull and kill all 3 named that respawn (100,000k HP each).<br>
6. Kill Coirnav.<br>
It might be possible to not kill the 3 named and root/CC them away from raid.<br>

<c "#0080ff">  < > < > Spells Info < > < > < > </c><br>
Curse of the Triumverate (-1000 Cold): Range 100 PBAE 2000 damage, -500 ATK, dispel.  Lasts one hour, 18 curse counters.<br>
