<c "#fc7703">  < > < > Boss Info < > < > < > </c><br>
Name: Solusek Ro<br>
Level: 80<br>
HP: 800,000<br>
Max Hit: 1800+<br>
<c "#0080ff">  < > < > Event Info < > < > < > </c><br>
Single target ramp, slowable.<br>
Buff fire resist.<br>
When you zone in up top you will be aggrod by two Guardian of Fire.  There are six total you need to clear.  They hit for 1300+ and are slowable.<br>
Ranged DPS should be able to outrange the AE (240 range, 150 AE range).<br>
Once dead, hail A Planar Projection and then drop down into Plane of Fire.<br>

<c "#0080ff">  < > < > Spells Info < > < > < > </c><br>
Solar Flame (-400 Fire): Single target 2000 DD.<br>
Solar Winds (-400 Fire): Targeted AE -1000 DD -50ATK.<br>
Corona of Sol (Self Buff): 150 DS, can be dispelled.<br>
