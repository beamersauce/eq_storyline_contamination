<c "#0080ff">Halls of Honor (HoHa)</c> <a href="https://everquest.allakhazam.com/db/zones.html?zstrat=171> - <a href="file:///storyline/PoP_Progression_Guide/HoHa.txt> HoH Story Window Boss Guide</a> - Zone Page</a> <a href="https://www.raspersrealm.com/Everquest/PoP/HoH_Events.html> - HoH Events Page</a><br>

<c "#ff3b3b>[_] Complete Trial 1 (North-west Trial - Save Villagers), then hail Rhaliq Trell to receive a flag emote:</c> <c "#fbff00">(An ethereal mist descends from the air and surrounds your very being.  Your body begins to emanate with the power of Rhaliq Trell.</c><br>
<c "#ff3b3b>[_] Complete Trial 1 (South-east Trial - Save Single Villager), then hail Alekson Garn to receive a flag emote:</c> <c "#fbff00">(An ethereal mist descends from the air and surrounds your very being.  Your body begins to emanate with the power of Alekson Garn.</c><br>

<c "#0080ff">Solusek Ro's Tower</c> <a href="https://everquest.allakhazam.com/db/zones.html?zstrat=164> - <a href="file:///storyline/PoP_Progression_Guide/SolRo.txt> Sol RO Story Window Boss Guide</a> - Zone Page</a> <a href="https://www.raspersrealm.com/Everquest/PoP/SolRo_Events.html> - Sol Ro Events Page</a> <br>

<c "#ff3b3b>[_] Kill Jiva </c> <c "#fbff00">(You've received a character flag!)</c><br>
<c "#ff3b3b>[_] Kill Rizlona </c> <c "#fbff00">(You've received a character flag!)</c><br>
<c "#ff3b3b>[_] Kill The Protector of Dresolik</c> <c "#fbff00">(You've received a character flag!)</c><br>
<c "#ff3b3b>[_] Kill Solusek Ro and hail A Planar Projection.</c> <c "#fbff00">(You've received a character flag!)</c><br>
